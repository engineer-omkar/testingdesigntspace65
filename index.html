About the founders

Welcome to my journey! My name is Tanmay Santosh Surse, and I have been passionate about graphic design since I was 9. By 14, I became a professional designer, fueled by my desire to bring creativity to life. I enrolled in graphic design classes, where I honed my skills & gained valuable experience working for various companies over five years. In 2018, 1 ventured into the graphic design business, allowing me to express my unique vision. This positive experience inspired me to launch my own company, Designtspace, in 2025, where I continue to create compelling designs tailored to my clients' needs. In my experience in companies and businesses, I found that so many clients can not fulfil their expectations from the company because of a lack of Knowledge of design so I decided to start my own company for the service of the client.
